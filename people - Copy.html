Harwinder Singh

I am Harwinder Singh, pursuing Master of Sciences in Electrical Engineering at the School of Engineering, San Francisco State University. I did my undergrad in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Maharishi Markandeshwar Engineering College (India) and 3 year diploma in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Pt. Jagat Ram Polytechnic, Punjab (India). As a graduate student of Dr. Mahmoodi, I have great experience. I have been working on aging effect and reliability of nanoscale devices from last 10 month. My thesis project is to analyze the combined effect of aging, temperature and process variations on the reliability of 6T SRAM in 32nm technology. I am interested in the designing and reliability issues of digital circuits (especially memory circuits) in the nanotechnology. My professional goals are to support the silicon technology to sustain reliably in the world of the nanotechnology devices and to bring the new technologies and the new materials in the fabrication process to welcome the Virtual Age.
