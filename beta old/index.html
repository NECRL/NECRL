Welcome to Nano- Electronics & Computing Research Center(NeCRC) at San Francisco State University

Waves of change in human history

Welcome to Nano-Electronics and Computing Research Center (NeCRC). The demand for much higher performance computing machines is never ending. Nano-electronics has a great promise in delivering the ever increasing demand for computational power set by increasing expansion of the information technology and its evolution towards the next wave of change in society, the Virtual Age.

Designing reliable, energy efficient, high performance computing hardware in emerging nanotechnologies is our mission at NeCRC. As the technology continues to scale down towards nanometer regimes, there are tremendous research opportunities to address the grand challenges and barriers of technology scaling. Our research addresses the technology scaling challenges at the circuit and architecture levels of abstraction.

We always welcome students who are interested in joining our research. Interested students, please contact Prof. Mahmoodi to learn more about current research opportunities to get involved.
