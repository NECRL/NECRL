Welcome to Nano- Electronics & Computing Research Laboratory (NeCRL) at San Francisco State University

Site Updated on April 29th 2016

Welcome to Nano-Electronics and Computing Research Laboratory (NeCRL). The demand for much higher performance computing machines is never ending. Nano-electronics has a great promise in delivering the ever increasing demand for computational power set by increasing expansion of the information technology and its evolution towards the next wave of change in society, the Virtual Age.

Designing reliable, energy efficient, high performance computing circuits in emerging nanotechnologies is our mission at NeCRL. As the technology continues to scale down towards nanometer regimes, there are tremendous research opportunities to address the grand challenges and barriers of technology scaling. Our research addresses the technology scaling challenges at the circuit and architecture levels of abstraction.
